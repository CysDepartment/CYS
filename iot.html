About Internet of Things

Internet of Things (IoT) is an innovative field that focuses on connecting physical devices to the internet to collect and exchange data. It plays a key role in the development of smart homes, smart cities, healthcare systems, and industrial automation. At Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST), the IoT Bachelor’s program prepares students with a strong foundation in embedded systems, wireless communication, cloud computing, and sensor networks. The program is delivered in English and consists of 132 credit hours, combining theoretical study with practical implementation. Graduates will be equipped to work in a variety of industries that rely on smart, connected technologies.
