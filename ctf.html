What is CTF?

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a cybersecurity competition where participants solve challenges to find hidden flags. These challenges test skills in areas such as cryptography, web exploitation, reverse engineering, and forensics.

CTFs are an excellent way to develop and showcase cybersecurity skills in a competitive and fun environment.
