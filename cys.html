About Cybersecurity Specialty

Cybersecurity (CYS) is a rapidly growing field that focuses on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital threats and cyberattacks. As technology becomes more integrated into every aspect of life, the demand for skilled cybersecurity professionals continues to rise. At Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST), the Bachelor’s program in Cybersecurity is designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience through advanced laboratories, real-world simulations, and industry-aligned curricula. The program is delivered in English and consists of 132 credit hours, covering key areas such as ethical hacking, network security, digital forensics, and information security management. Graduates will be well-prepared to pursue careers in government, private sector, or continue with advanced studies in the field.
