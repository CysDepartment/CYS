About Computer Games Design & Development

Computer Game Design and Development (CGDD) is an exciting field that combines creativity, storytelling, and advanced computing to build interactive digital games. The demand for skilled game developers continues to grow with the expansion of the entertainment and simulation industries. At Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST), the program focuses on key areas such as game programming, 3D modeling, graphics design, artificial intelligence, and virtual reality. The Bachelor’s degree consists of 132 credit hours, and is taught in English, providing students with the technical and artistic skills needed to create engaging and immersive gaming experiences. Graduates can pursue careers in game development studios, multimedia production, or independent game design.
